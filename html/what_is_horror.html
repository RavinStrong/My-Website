What Is Horror?

Definition of horrorr

Horror is actually quite a broad genre. the main definition is it has to be a piece of fiction that shocks or even frightens the viewer, often conjuring up feelings of repulsion, shock or... horror. Horror encapsulated many sub-genres including but not limited to, thriller, psychological, gothic, slasher, supernatural and even comedic horror.

Horror misconceptions

One common misconception of horror is that it is similar or akin to true crime. True crime is a soecific genre that is based of real events telling the stories of real victims and real perpetrators. While some horror has been loosly inspired by true events, horror is explicitly fictitious. When it comes to true crime there is often ethical concerns about exploiting victims and their families or even idiolizing relal criminals. In horror this is not the case even when fans seem to "idiolize" a villian it s a fictitious character who caused no real harm.
